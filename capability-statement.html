Company Overview

Empiral, LLC is a U.S. small business focused on responsive sourcing, procurement support, and commercial product supply for federal, defense, and civilian agencies. Empiral supports straightforward, time-sensitive buying needs with disciplined supplier outreach, quote preparation, and fulfillment coordination.

Core Capabilities

  • Commercial product sourcing and supply support
  • Office supplies, consumables, and operational items
  • IT peripherals and common COTS categories
  • Janitorial and cleaning supplies
  • PPE and safety item sourcing
  • Hardware and MRO item sourcing
  • Supplier outreach, pricing collection, and comparison tracking
  • Buyer-focused documentation and quote support

Differentiators

  • Same-day quote turnaround on incoming RFQs
  • Fast-response operating model for simplified acquisitions and micro-purchases
  • Clear, factual communication with agencies and suppliers
  • Flexible sourcing across multiple commercial categories
  • Government Purchase Card accepted; ACH / EFT and WAWF invoicing supported

Target Buyers / Use Cases

  • Defense Logistics Agency buying offices
  • DoD and civilian buyers needing commercial item support
  • Micro-purchase and simplified acquisition workflows
  • Fast-turn RFQs requiring supplier outreach and quote preparation

Payment and Ordering

  • Government Purchase Card (GPC) accepted
  • ACH / EFT and WAWF invoicing supported
  • Agency-approved payment workflows

Relevant commercial sourcing and procurement experience available upon request.
